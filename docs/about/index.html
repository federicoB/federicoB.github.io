About

on 2025-11-15

Hello! My name is Federico, and welcome to my personal blog.

I am passionate about technology, programming, and problem-solving. In my daily work, I focus on digitalization and simplifying processes, especially in public services. I enjoy reverse engineering, tinkering with hardware and software, and learning new skills along the way.

This blog is a space where I share:

  • Technical experiments: From software projects to home automation setups.
  • Travel stories and photography: Documenting experiences and memories.
  • Thoughts and reflections: On productivity, learning, and life in general.

I aim for clarity, practicality, and honesty in everything I write. If you enjoy deep dives into technology or simple life insights, you’ll feel right at home here.

Feel free to explore my posts, and if you want to reach out, you can contact me via GitHub.

Thank you for visiting!
